About Us

Shining Light Mission is a Christian non-governmental organization dedicated to mentoring, protecting, and empowering teenagers and young adults. We believe every young person deserves guidance, support, and the opportunity to discover their God-given purpose. Through faith-based programs, community outreach, and personal mentorship, we strive to inspire hope, build character, and nurture the next generation of leaders. Our team is passionate about creating safe spaces where youth can grow spiritually, emotionally, and intellectually, making a positive impact in their communities and beyond.

Our Mission

To guide, protect, and mentor young people, helping them discover their God-given purpose and equipping them for a bright future. We are committed to providing holistic support that fosters spiritual growth, emotional resilience, and practical life skills through Christ-centered mentorship and community engagement.

Our Vision

To see a generation of empowered, faith-filled young people making a positive impact in their communities and the world. We envision a future where every teenager and young adult is equipped with the values, confidence, and opportunities to shine as a light in society, reflecting the love and hope of Christ.
